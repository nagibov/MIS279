Evolution & Milestones

  • 1990s: Early online stores, SSL, the first carts
  • 2000s: Marketplaces & payments (PayPal, fraud/risk standards)
  • 2010s: Mobile commerce, social selling, logistics platforms
  • 2020s: AI search/reco, subscriptions, AR try‑ons, same‑day

Business Models

Lecture 1.2

Market Research: Why It Matters

  • De‑risk ideas with data before building
  • Quantify demand; identify underserved segments
  • Translate findings into a clear value proposition
Lecture 1.2

Core Tools & Signals

  • Google Trends for demand patterns & seasonality
  • Similarweb for competitor traffic & channels
  • Statista / Think with Google for market sizing
  • Social listening: Reddit, TikTok, Instagram for pain points

  • Pick a category (fashion, food, tech, services)
  • Use Google Trends to compare top 3 terms
  • Audit 2 competitors with Similarweb (sources & geo)
  • Draft 3 pain points + 1 hypothesis statement
  • Prepare a 2‑minute pitch for next class
